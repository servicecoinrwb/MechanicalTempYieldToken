Executive Summary

MTYLD (Mechanical Temp Yield) is a digital asset designed exclusively for the stakeholders of Mechanical Temp. Unlike traditional cash bonuses which lose purchasing power over time, MTYLD is a backed asset that grows in value through the company's active treasury management and revenue injection strategies.

MTYLD holders gain exclusive access to The Conservator — an AI-powered ETH savings vault that autonomously deploys capital into yield strategies on Arbitrum. This gives holders a second way to grow their wealth, denominated in ETH.

MTYLD holders also gain access to the MT Business Credit Line — an on-chain private credit facility where both MTYLD and mtETH-S can be used as collateral to back business operations and earn a share of interest.

Think of MTYLD as a high-yield savings account combined with company stock, but with instant liquidity. Think of The Conservator as your AI-managed ETH savings account. Think of the Credit Line as the company's on-chain bank.

How MTYLD Works

The value of MTYLD is derived from multiple revenue streams, ensuring growth even when HVAC seasonality varies.

1

Real Revenue (HVAC)

A portion of Mechanical Temp's physical service revenue is converted into USDC and injected into the vault regularly, directly increasing every token's value.

2

Treasury Yield (DeFi)

Idle treasury funds are actively deployed into diverse, high-grade strategies:

  • xGRAIL Dividends: Daily yield from Camelot DEX ecosystem participation.
  • USUAL Rewards: Weekly rewards from stablecoin infrastructure.
  • Cypher Streaming: Continuous yield from protocol streaming incentives.
3

Credit Line Interest

When Mechanical Temp draws on the business credit line, interest accrues at 8% APR. 30% of that interest flows to collateral depositors (MTYLD and mtETH-S holders) proportional to their USD collateral value.

4

NAV Injection

Combined earnings are injected into the vault, increasing the Net Asset Value (NAV) of every MTYLD token. The token can only go up relative to USDC.

The Result: Your MTYLD grows from HVAC work, DeFi treasury management, and credit line interest — simultaneously.

The Conservator — mtETH-S

Exclusively available to MTYLD holders. Your MTYLD token is your key to access.

The Conservator is an autonomous AI agent managing an ERC-4626 ETH vault on Arbitrum One. It monitors the vault around the clock and deploys idle WETH into Aave V3 to earn yield — without any manual intervention required.

1

Deposit WETH

Connect your wallet to the Conservator dashboard at vault.mechanicaltemp.com. Approve and deposit WETH. Minimum deposit is 0.01 WETH.

2

Receive mtETH-S Shares

The vault mints mtETH-S tokens to your wallet representing your ownership share. Share count stays fixed — but each share becomes worth more WETH as yield accrues.

3

AI Deploys to Aave

The Conservator agent automatically detects idle WETH and deploys it to Aave V3, keeping a small buffer for liquidity. Yield accrues every block.

4

Use mtETH-S as Collateral

While earning Aave yield, your mtETH-S can also be deposited into the MT Credit Line as collateral at 60% LTV — earning a share of loan interest on top of the existing Aave yield.

What is NAV?

Net Asset Value — how much WETH each mtETH-S share is worth. Starts near 1.0 and grows as yield accrues. Your position = shares × NAV.

What is mtETH-S?

Your share token. Received when depositing WETH. Burned when withdrawing. Holds your claim to vault assets including accumulated yield.

Withdraw Lock

A 24-hour lock applies after each deposit to prevent flash loan attacks. Plan withdrawals accordingly.

AI Agent Safety

The agent can only interact with whitelisted contracts approved by the owner. It cannot send funds to arbitrary addresses.

MT Business Credit Line v3

The on-chain bank of Mechanical Temp. Three roles — USDC depositor, collateral provider, or borrower.

The credit line is a three-sided market. USDC depositors fund the pool. MTYLD and mtETH-S holders post collateral to back loans. Mechanical Temp borrows USDC for real business operations and repays with interest — which flows back to all participants.

A

USDC Depositors — Earn 70% of Interest

Anyone can deposit USDC to fund the lending pool. When a loan is repaid, 70% of interest distributes proportionally to USDC depositors. Withdraw freely when no loan is active.

B

mtETH-S Collateral — 60% LTV • Share of 30% Interest

Deposit mtETH-S vault shares as collateral. Each mtETH-S contributes 60% of its ETH value to the borrowing power. While locked, it continues earning Aave yield inside the Conservator vault. Earn a proportional share of the 30% collateral interest split.

C

MTYLD Collateral — 70% LTV • Share of 30% Interest

MTYLD receives the highest LTV (70%) because its price is USDC-stable — backed by real HVAC revenue, not crypto market movements. MTYLD collateral earns the same proportional share of the 30% interest pool. The 30% split between mtETH-S and MTYLD is weighted by each pool's USD value at repayment time.
