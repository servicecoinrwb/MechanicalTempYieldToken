Executive Summary

MTYLD (Mechanical Temp Yield) is a digital asset designed exclusively for the stakeholders of Mechanical Temp. Unlike traditional cash bonuses which lose purchasing power over time, MTYLD is a backed asset that grows in value daily through the company's active treasury management and revenue injection strategies.

MTYLD holders also gain exclusive access to The Conservator — an AI-powered ETH savings vault that autonomously deploys capital into yield strategies on Arbitrum. This gives holders a second way to grow their wealth, denominated in ETH.

Think of MTYLD as a high-yield savings account combined with company stock, but with instant liquidity. Think of The Conservator as your AI-managed ETH savings account.

How MTYLD Works

The value of MTYLD is derived from multiple revenue streams, ensuring growth even when HVAC seasonality varies.

1

Real Revenue (HVAC)

A portion of Mechanical Temp's physical service revenue is converted into USDC and injected into the vault monthly.

2

Treasury Yield (DeFi)

Idle treasury funds are actively deployed into diverse, high-grade strategies to generate additional revenue:

  • xGRAIL Dividends: Daily yield from Camelot DEX ecosystem participation.
  • USUAL Rewards: Weekly rewards from stablecoin infrastructure.
  • Cypher Streaming: Continuous yield from protocol streaming incentives.
3

Daily NAV Injection

Combined earnings are injected into the vault daily, increasing the Net Asset Value (NAV) of every MTYLD token in circulation.

The Result: If you hold MTYLD, your balance grows from both HVAC work and sophisticated treasury management.

The Conservator — mtETH-S

Exclusively available to MTYLD holders. Your MTYLD token is your key to access.

The Conservator is an autonomous AI agent managing an ERC-4626 ETH vault on Arbitrum One. It monitors the vault around the clock and deploys idle WETH into Aave V3 to earn yield — without any manual intervention required.

1

Deposit WETH

Connect your wallet to the Conservator dashboard at vault.mechanicaltemp.com. Approve and deposit WETH. Minimum deposit is 0.01 WETH.

2

Receive mtETH-S Shares

When you deposit, the vault mints mtETH-S tokens to your wallet. These tokens represent your ownership share of the vault. The number of shares you receive depends on the current NAV — not 1:1 with WETH.

3

AI Deploys to Aave

The Conservator agent automatically detects idle WETH in the vault and deploys it to Aave V3, keeping a small buffer for liquidity. Yield accrues every block.

4

NAV Grows Over Time

As Aave earns yield, the total assets in the vault grow. Your mtETH-S shares stay the same count but each share becomes worth more WETH. When you withdraw, you receive more WETH than you deposited.

What is NAV?

Net Asset Value — how much WETH each mtETH-S share is worth. Starts near 1.0 and grows as yield accrues. Your position value = shares × NAV.

What is mtETH-S?

Your share token. Received when depositing WETH. Burned when withdrawing. Holds your claim to vault assets including all accumulated yield.

Withdraw Lock

A 24-hour lock applies after each deposit. This prevents flash loan attacks. Plan withdrawals accordingly.

AI Agent Safety

The agent can only interact with whitelisted contracts approved by the owner. It cannot send funds to arbitrary addresses or steal deposits.
