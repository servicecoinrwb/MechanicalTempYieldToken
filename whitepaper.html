MTYLD

Employee & Partner Asset Program

Profit Sharing • Active Treasury • Private Credit

Executive Summary

MTYLD (Mechanical Temp Yield) is a digital asset designed exclusively for the stakeholders of Mechanical Temp. Unlike traditional cash bonuses which lose purchasing power over time, MTYLD is a backed asset that grows in value daily through the company's active treasury management and revenue injection strategies.

Think of it as a high-yield savings account combined with company stock, but with instant liquidity.

The Partner Portal

We have removed the complexity of traditional crypto tools. Accessing your assets is now as simple as logging into your email.

No seed phrases. Our system creates a secure, self-custodial wallet for you instantly using your company email.

Buy with Card

Direct integration with MoonPay. Buy USDC directly to your portal wallet using a debit or credit card.

How It Works

The value of MTYLD is derived from multiple revenue streams, ensuring growth even when HVAC seasonality varies.

1

Real Revenue (HVAC)

A portion of Mechanical Temp's physical service revenue is converted into USDC and injected into the vault monthly.

2

Treasury Yield (DeFi)

Idle treasury funds are actively deployed into diverse, high-grade strategies to generate additional revenue:

  • xGRAIL Dividends: Daily yield from Camelot DEX ecosystem participation.
  • USUAL Rewards: Weekly rewards from stablecoin infrastructure.
  • Cypher Streaming: Continuous yield from protocol streaming incentives.
3

Daily Injection

We inject these combined earnings into the Vault. This increases the Net Asset Value (NAV) of every token in circulation.

The Result: If you hold MTYLD, your balance grows from both HVAC work and sophisticated treasury management.

Why Participate?

1. Superior Growth

Your MTYLD tokens are backed by an actively managed treasury. The goal is to outperform traditional bank interest rates significantly, allowing your bonus to work as hard as you do.

2. Instant Liquidity

You are never locked in. Unlike a 401k or stock options that vest over years, you can redeem your MTYLD for USDC (cash equivalent) instantly, 24/7, through our secure web portal.

3. Stability

MTYLD is not a volatile cryptocurrency like Bitcoin. It is backed by stable assets (USDC). The mechanism is designed to go up only relative to the treasury backing.

The "Bank of Mechanical Temp"

Don't sell your upside just because you need cash.

We have built an internal Private Credit Line for our partners.

  • Borrow Against Your Bonus: If you have an unexpected expense, you can deposit your MTYLD as collateral and borrow cash (USDC) instantly.
  • Keep Earning: While your MTYLD is deposited as collateral, it continues to grow in value.
  • Simple Terms: No credit checks. No paperwork. The blockchain handles the collateralization automatically.
