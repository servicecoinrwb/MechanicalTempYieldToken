How does the yield work?
Mechanical Temp generates revenue from real-world HVAC contracts. A portion is converted to USDC and injected into the vault, increasing the NAV of every MTYLD token.
Is my deposit locked?
No. You can redeem MTYLD for USDC at any time, unless the vault is in Epoch Close mode.
What is the First Mint tax?
To protect against inflation attacks, the first depositor burns a tiny amount of shares (1000 wei, less than $0.000001). Standard security practice.
