How the Credit Line Works

1

USDC depositors fund the pool

Anyone deposits USDC to create available credit for Mechanical Temp to draw from.

2

mtETH-S holders back it with collateral

MTYLD holders lock Conservator shares (70% LTV via Chainlink). mtETH-S keeps earning Compound yield while locked.

3

Mechanical Temp draws USDC

Borrows up to 70% of collateral value for up to 90 days at 8% APR for operations, parts, and payroll.

4

Repayment distributes interest

Principal + interest repaid on-chain. 70% of interest to USDC depositors, 30% to collateral providers.
