① The Vault (Smart Contract)

A secure on-chain safe on Arbitrum. It holds WETH (wrapped ETH). When you deposit WETH, you get mtETH-S back. These shares represent your ownership slice of everything in the vault.

② The Conservator (AI Agent)

The AI running on your computer (ElizaOS + Claude). It watches the vault and can move funds into approved yield strategies like Compound — but only ones you've whitelisted. It cannot steal funds.

③ This Dashboard

Your control panel. You deposit WETH, withdraw back to WETH, approve which strategies the AI can use, and rotate the AI agent wallet. Only the owner wallet can approve strategies.

💡 The Money Flow — Plain English
User Deposits WETH → Vault mints mtETH-S → AI deploys to Compound → Yield grows NAV → Withdraw = more WETH than you put in
🔐 What is NAV / Share Price?

NAV = Net Asset Value. It represents how much WETH each mtETH-S share is worth. As Compound earns yield, NAV goes up. Your mtETH-S balance stays the same but each share is worth more WETH over time. Your position value = shares × NAV.

⏱ What is the Withdraw Lock?

After depositing, you must wait 24 hours before withdrawing. This is a security feature built into the contract — it prevents flash loan attacks. Plan accordingly.
