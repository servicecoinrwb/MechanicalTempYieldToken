FAQ

Do I need crypto to join the waitlist?
No. The waitlist is email-based. If/when you're whitelisted, we'll send simple steps.
Is this a credit card purchase?
No. We're not offering card payments directly on this site. Access is gated via whitelist and on-chain flows. However, the Partner Portal supports MoonPay for approved users.
What are the risks?
Business performance, smart contract risk, and market conditions. NAV is not fixed; it reflects treasury per token.
What is mtETH-S and how is it different from MTYLD?
MTYLD is backed by real HVAC revenue converted to USDC. mtETH-S is a separate ETH savings vault — a member benefit for MTYLD holders. You deposit WETH, receive mtETH-S shares, and The Conservator AI manages that ETH to earn yield. They are two separate products that work together.
