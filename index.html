How It Works

1. Mint Projects

New **Mechanical Temp** HVAC projects are verified and submitted as "Work Orders." `$MTYLD` tokens are minted against their projected future revenue.

2. Back with Capital

Investors buy the newly minted `$MTYLD` with **USDC**, providing the capital that funds the parts and labor for the HVAC projects.

3. Earn Yield

As HVAC projects are completed and paid by the customer, that revenue is paid back to the contract, increasing the **USDC** balance and the value of all `$MTYLD`.
