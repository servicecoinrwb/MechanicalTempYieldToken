How MTYLD Works

Plain-language overview—no crypto experience required.

1) Real Work → USDC

We complete HVAC jobs. A portion of revenue is converted to USDC and queued in the on-chain vault.

2) Active Treasury Management

Idle treasury funds are actively deployed into low-risk, yield-bearing stablecoin strategies. This ensures consistent NAV growth.

3) Hold or Redeem

Hold MTYLD to participate in treasury growth, or redeem MTYLD back to USDC at the current NAV.
