Do I need crypto to join the waitlist?
No. The waitlist is email-based. If/when you're whitelisted, we'll send simple steps.
Can I buy crypto with a card?
Yes — use our Stripe onramp above to buy ETH or USDC with a credit card, debit card, or bank transfer. No exchange account needed.
What are the risks?
Business performance, smart contract risk, and market conditions. NAV is not fixed; it reflects treasury per token.
What is mtETH-S and how is it different from MTYLD?
MTYLD is backed by real HVAC revenue converted to USDC. mtETH-S is a separate ETH savings vault — a member benefit for MTYLD holders. They are two separate products that work together.
