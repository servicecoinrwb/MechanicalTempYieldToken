How It Works

1. Propose & Mint

Mechanical Temp proposes new HVAC jobs via the contract. After a **safety delay**, `$MTYLD` tokens representing **10% of the job's value** (less a 5% reserve on that 10%) are minted.

2. Back with Capital

Investors buy the newly minted `$MTYLD` with **USDC**, providing the capital that funds the tokenized portion of the HVAC projects.

3. Earn Yield

As projects are completed, Mechanical Temp pays back the **10% tokenized value** in USDC to the contract. This increases the USDC balance backing all `$MTYLD` tokens, generating yield.
